Hello! I'm Laurentiu, front-end developer and interaction designer from Bucharest, Romania.

I strongly believe that interaction design is not a math problem with one correct answer and my goal for every project is to craft a modern digital experience that has some thoughtful details, is functional, reliable and pleasurable without sacrificing the commercial requirements.

Over the past 6+ years I have delivered code or design for companies like Apa Bucovina, J.F. Lehman & Co, Dynasoft Synergy, METRO Ro, KC Deco, Okapi Studio, Perspecsys Cloud Data Protection, George Mason University, Raiffeisen Bank Romania and Aqua Carpatica or for romanian celebrities like Loredana Groza, Cabral, Andreea Patrascu and Andi Moisescu.

I am currently available for commission enquiries.
